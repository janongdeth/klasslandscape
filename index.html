How It Works

Here's what a Klass job looks like from start to finish.

  1. 01.

    Site walk and written estimate

    The owner comes out, looks at the job with you, and writes the scope on paper. No sales rep, no pressure, no mystery pricing.

  2. 02.

    Plan and protect

    We mark drop zones, lay plywood over what needs protecting, and rig anything that comes down in sections. Your landscaping, fence, and driveway stay where they are.

  3. 03.

    Cut and clear

    Every cut is staged. Brush goes to the chipper, logs to the truck, debris to the trailer. We don't leave a lot looking like a logging deck.

  4. 04.

    Rake out and final walk

    Sawdust raked, ruts smoothed, chips swept off the pavement. We walk the property with you before loading up. If something's not right, we fix it before we leave.
