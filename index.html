Section C.

Field Notes — how a Klass job runs.

  1. 01.

    Site walk & written estimate

    The owner comes out, walks the lot with you, and writes the scope on paper. No salesman, no pressure, no ten-day “limited time” nonsense.

  2. 02.

    Plan & protect

    We mark drop zones, lay plywood over what shouldn’t take traffic, and tie off anything that needs rigging. Your beds, your fence, and your dog stay where they are.

  3. 03.

    Cut & clear

    Every cut is staged. Brush goes to the chipper, logs to the truck, debris to the trailer. We don’t leave the lot looking like a logging deck.

  4. 04.

    Rake out & final walk

    Sawdust raked, ruts smoothed, chips swept off the drive. We walk the property with you before you sign — if it isn’t right, we fix it before we load up.
