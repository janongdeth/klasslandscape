The same person who quotes it runs it.

Klass Tree and Landscape is a small, owner-operated tree care and landscaping company based in Fort Wayne, Indiana. We take work within 50 miles of the shop - residential, commercial, and the occasional farmstead clearing job.

There's no call center, no subcontracted crew, no sales rep who disappears after you sign. The person who walked your property and wrote the estimate is the same person operating the equipment on the day of the job. That's been the model since day one and it's not changing.

We've been doing this work in northeast Indiana and northwest Ohio long enough to know that the jobs people remember aren't the straightforward ones. It's the tree that's hanging over a roof in January, the crane job on a lot with no room to maneuver, the storm call at 5am. Those are the jobs that define whether you actually know what you're doing. We do.

We're a TCIA member - the Tree Care Industry Association sets the safety and training standards for professional tree care. Our equipment is owner-operated, not rented for the week. Full general liability and workers' comp, certificate on request before we start.
