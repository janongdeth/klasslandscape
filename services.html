Common Questions

Quick answers from the owner. If you don't see what you need, call 260-443-1666.

How much does tree removal cost in Fort Wayne?

Most residential tree removals in the Fort Wayne area run between $400 and $2,500 depending on tree size, location, and access. We give every customer a free written estimate before any work starts. Hazardous removals near power lines or structures cost more because they require crane work or specialty rigging.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes. Klass Tree and Landscape carries full liability and workers' comp insurance, and we are a TCIA member. We can send certificates directly to your insurer or property manager on request.

Do you respond to storm damage emergencies?

Yes, 24/7. After a storm we pick up the phone, document the damage for your insurance adjuster, and dispatch a crew as soon as the scene is safe. Trees on roofs, vehicles, or driveways are our priority calls.

What areas do you serve?

We work within a 50-mile radius of Fort Wayne, Indiana. That covers Allen, DeKalb, Whitley, Wells, Adams, Huntington, Wabash, and Noble counties in Indiana, plus parts of northwest Ohio including Defiance, Paulding, and Van Wert counties.

Do you grind the stump after removal?

Stump grinding is a separate service we quote alongside removal. Most customers add it; some leave the stump for use as a planter or marker. Either way it's clearly priced on the estimate, no surprises.

How fast can you give me an estimate?

The owner reads every request himself and usually calls back the same day. Most on-site estimates happen within 48 hours of your call. Storm and emergency requests get same-day response.
